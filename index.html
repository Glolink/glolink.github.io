Learning

Support organizational development by ensuring the overall quality of the development process for learning environments at different levels through the use of technology.

Innovation

Innovation is a key to the success of organizations and societies. The decisive factor is whether an innovation can be used and exploited in different markets.

Knowledge Management

Knowledge management is a key to successfully initiating global innovation processes and leads to long-term and knowledge-intensive innovation partnerships.
