Some Awesome Projects

ProNet Handwerk (2020-2024) funding from the BMBF:
The project aims at creating collaborative educational offers between universities and educational institutions in the craft industry in the field of Smart Homes / Buildings. 

FabCitizen (2020-2023) funding from EU, Erasmus+:  
The goal of the Erasmus+ project FabCitizen is to develop competencies for Citizen Science projects in schools. 

2019 

Computational Thinking and Acting COTA

The goal of the Erasmus+ project CoTA (Computational Thinking and Acting) is to develop and validate new learning and teaching ICT solutions in Computational Thinking and problem solving in primary schools.

2016-2018

Emscher-Lippe hoch 4 (EL4) and Positive Computing

EL4 funding from EFRE NRW, the Emscher-Lippe hoch 4 research project is investigating solutions to the challenges of digitization in the modern world of work, particularly in FabLabs.

Positive Computing investigates and considers in system design the effect of technologies on people in terms of basic psychological needs such as autonomy, belonging, and understanding, and thus on motivation and well-being.
