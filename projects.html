ProNet Handwerk

The project aims at creating collaborative educational offers between universities and educational institutions in the craft industry in the field of Smart Homes / Buildings. From a research perspective, new educational methods as well as competency frameworks will be developed.

HRWgoesCOIL

FabCitizen

The goal of the Erasmus+ project FabCitizen is to develop competencies for Citizen Science projects in schools. As a first part, a comprehensive competency framework will be developed for teachers and students (grade 5-9). As a second step, learning scenarios will be developed and validated in different schools across Europe

Computational Thinking and Acting COTA

The goal of the Erasmus+ project CoTA (Computational Thinking and Acting) is to develop and validate new learning and teaching ICT solutions in Computational Thinking and problem solving in primary schools. CoTA will also develop scenarios for including programming into different subjects and a competence framework of Physical Computing linked to existing national curricula in Finland, Estonia, Germany and Greece.

Emscher Lippe4

The Emscher-Lippe hoch 4 research project is investigating solutions to the challenges of digitization in the modern working world, particularly in FabLabs. The project views digitization as a social process rather than a purely technological one. The goal is to explore processes at a wide variety of levels and derive recommendations for action for the region.

Positive Computing

Positive Computing investigates and considers in system design the effect of technologies on people in terms of basic psychological needs such as autonomy, belonging, and understanding, and thus on motivation and well-being.

Completed Projects :

ÖWR: Öffentliche Wissensressourcen With a view to the potentials of OER and the current demand situation of public administrations, the project aims to develop and test a comprehensive introduction and competence development for the use of OER. Duration (11.2016-05.2018), Funding from BMBF, Partners: Duale Hochschule Baden Württemberg (DHBW), Kommunale Gemeinschaftsstelle für Verwaltungsmanagement (KGSt), Verwaltungsakademie Bordesholm (VAB)

EAGLE: Enhanced Government E-Learning The project EAGLE develops an Open Learning Platform and Services which are adapted for rural local governments. Funding from EU FP7, duration (02.2014-01.2017), Partners:DHBW, Dublin City University, Fraunhofer Fokus, and 6 further partners 

Play4Guidance: A European Business Game to train and guide students and young unemployed on entrepreneurial, transversal and mathematical skills. Play4Guidance aims to bring the world of education and training in close contact with the job market, in order to match school and university curricula to the market’s real needs. Duration (2014-2017)
