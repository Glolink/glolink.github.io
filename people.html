Current Members

We are a group of people led by Prof. Dr. Jan Pawlowski. We aim to promote research collaboration in the field of Information Systems.

Jan Pawlowski has been teaching and researching Information Systems at Hochschule Ruhr West since January 1, 2014. he was Professor of Global Information Systems at the University of Jyväskylä in Finland. He is involved in various organisations and committees such as the Scientific Commission on Information Systems (WKWI), IEEE, AIS and CEN.
